WEEK 4:

What did you learn today?

We looked into planning user journeys, and flexbox within CSS.

What was new / interesting?

Flexbox was a new concept for me to learn, it allows you to order/ lay elements out within a container in a myriad of ways. It is also useful for responsive web-design.

How can it be applied in web design?

We looked into user-flows and customer journey maps (CJM). These are effective tools to provide an optimal experience throughout the entire time a user is on a website so there are no poor areas of customer experience.

How does it influence you as a web-designer?

Customer journey maps and userflows will influence the way I see and design the overall experience of a website.
